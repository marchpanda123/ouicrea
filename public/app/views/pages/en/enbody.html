About OuiCrea

Chine-Hexagone Accelerator

OuiCrea ambition is to establish a cross-boundary ecosystem of innovation and entrepreneurship between China and France.

At the meantime, OuiCrea specializes in researching and monitoring ‘unicorn’ projects that can bring and accelerate technological breakthroughs and has attracted a lot of investors. ---- ‘unicorn project’

OuiCrea will focus on the following departments: TMT, culture and creativity connections, objects and intelligence, etc. Through these high-quality resources and our entrepreneurs in France and China, we help realize the dream of entrepreneurs with respect to the following areas :

  • Make acquaintance with Franco-Chinese entrepreneurs
  • Visit freely and liberally our sites Paris / Suzhou / Shanghai
  • Learn of policies concerning the innovation in China et in France
  • Organize matchmaking for clients, suppliers, partners and investors
  • Receive advices and assistances offered by our experts and professionals
  • Attract potentials investors
