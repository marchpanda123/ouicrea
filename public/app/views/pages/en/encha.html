Cross-border incubation

China-Hexagon Accelerator (CHA) is an acceleration program designed by OuiCrea in 2017 based on its own expertise for entrepreneurs and cross-border startups, which is the core business of the OuiCrea ecosystem.

The project is based on the OuiCrea CHA @ Station F space, helping innovative French companies to understand the Chinese market and try to enter it, also helping Chinese innovative companies to develop activities in Europe.

Indeed, the China-Accelerator Hexagon program is called the 6 + 6 program, which consists of 6 months of incubation and 6 months of acceleration in the two countries respectively.

1st month

Installation at YesCrea CHA (Station F)
Business plan and presentation training (Arrival and installation at OuiCrea CHA (Station F) Training on Business plan and presentation)

2nd month

Evaluation and improvement of the business plan
Oral pitch (connection with a contest)

3rd month

Workshops: know the Chinese market
Chinese market study with Chinese students in France

4th month

Expedition to China, business trip

5th month

Offshoring preparation in China
Business Skills Training in China (2 hours)
Training on Chinese culture and basic language (2 hours)

6th month

Administration process for installation in China
Registration of the company in China
Website building, Cloud service

7th - 12th month:

Incubation at YesCrea in China (Suzhou / Shanghai) or in a partner incubator
